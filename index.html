Theme Shots of Durga Puja

Pre-wedding photographers in Kolkata use Dhunuchi dance poses, festive backgrounds, and bold colors to present the look and feel of Durga Puja, the most prominent festival in Kolkata. The going-to-bride wears a White-Red traditional-style Bengali saree with a bindi in the middle of the forehead and Shakha Pola in the hands with Alta. On the other hand, the going-to-be groom wears a traditional Dhoti Punjabi.

Vintage Shots

There are various locations in Kolkata where you can get a vintage feel. The places include Kolkata Raj Bari, Victoria Memorial, Howrah Bridge, Princep Ghat, Eco Park, Rabindra Sarovar, and so on.

Cinematic Storytelling and Candid

These days, capturing candid, stolen glances, playful moments, laughter, and Bollywood romance style is trending for pre-wedding photoshoots.

AI and AR Effects

With the latest innovations like artificial intelligence and augmented reality, photographers are using these effects to create floating flower beds and fantasy backgrounds for a perfect pre-wedding photoshoot in Kolkata.

Selfie and Mirror Photo-booths

Wedding photographers in Kolkata are aware of the latest trends and follow them. These days, unposed photos are trending to be captured by Polaroid cameras. Smartphone selfies and mirror selfies with short messages are trending.
